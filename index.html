How it works

Order. Watch. Wow.

Every cone is a 90-second performance — and the crowd it draws is the point.

STEP 01

Guest taps & pays

Touchscreen ordering with card, Apple Pay, and Google Pay built in. Guests pick a shape and color combination from 30+ designs. No cash handling, no line staff.

STEP 02

The robot spins it live

A robotic arm sculpts the cotton candy into a 3D shape behind glass, in full view. Crowds gather, phones come out — the machine is the attraction.

STEP 03

Fresh cone, happy guest

In under 90 seconds the finished creation is dispensed — photographed, posted, and tagged with your venue before the first bite.
